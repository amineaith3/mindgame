A new game
with a new concept

Going with only numbers,

1 : red

2 : green

3 : peachpuff

4 : yellow

5 : blue

6 : purple

The program generates a random position of 4 colors that may be repeated from the 6 ones above.
And you will have to find out the colors and their positions.

Everything else will be shown in an alert window.
